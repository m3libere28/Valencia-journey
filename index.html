Digital Nomad Visa Guide

Everything you need for your DNV application

1 General Documentation

Application Forms

  • **National Visa Application Form**: Completed and signed by the main applicant.
  • **Form EX-01**: Completed and signed.
  • **Passport**: Original with validity of 1 year left. You'll need scanned copies of each page.

Translations & Apostilles

  • **Criminal Background Check**: Must be translated by a sworn translator and have a Hague Apostille.
  • **Birth Certificate**: Required for all family members, must be translated by a sworn translator and have a Hague Apostille.
  • **Marriage Certificate**: Required for spouses, must be translated by a sworn translator and have a Hague Apostille.

2 Financial Documentation

You must prove sufficient and stable income to support your family. Your projected monthly income of **$8,188.00** comfortably exceeds the DNV minimum.

Required Documents:

  • Bank statements from the last 12 months.
  • VA disability income letters to prove consistent income.
  • Proof of investment income, if applicable.
  • **Proof of Administrative Fee 790 038:** Proof of payment is required for the initial permit.

3 Other Key Documents

In addition to the above, you will need a few more documents to complete your application.

  • **Medical Certificate:** Required for all applicants to state that you do not have any diseases that may cause serious repercussions for public health. Must be translated by a sworn translator.
  • **Proof of Health Insurance:** Certificate of public or private health insurance valid in Spain.
  • **Letter of Intent:** A letter explaining your purpose for the move.
  • **Letter of Employment:** A letter from Emily's company stating she is a remote worker.

4 The Application Process

You will need to submit your application in person at the Consulate, but it's important to remember that the final approval is handled by Spanish authorities.

  • **Book an appointment:** You must book a physical appointment at the Consulate to submit your application and documents.
  • **Online Application:** The online application on the UGE (Spanish authority) website requires a digital certificate and NIE number, which is why a relocation specialist is often recommended.
